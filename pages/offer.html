How to get updates about PharmEasy offers and discounts?

Visit the Offers section on the PharmEasy website to explore the latest offers and discounts. You can also download the PharmEasy app from Google Play or Apple App Store to receive notifications on offers and discounts.


How do I use a coupon code on PharmEasy?

Follow these steps to use a coupon code on PharmEasy –

  • Download the PharmEasy app or visit the website.
  • Select the products you want and add them to the cart.
  • Visit the cart page and click on Apply Coupon.
  • Enter the coupon code and click Apply.
  • Proceed to checkout to avail the discount.

    • Do I get a special discount when ordering for the 1st time?

    Yes, PharmEasy offers a special discount when you place your 1st order. Additionally, you also get exciting wallet offers and cashbacks.


    Do I get a special discount when ordering for the 1st time?

    How to a cancel/promo code?

    To cancel a coupon/promo code, you have to follow these steps –

    • Go to the cart page.
    • Click on ‘Remove’ in the coupon section to remove the coupon/promo code.
